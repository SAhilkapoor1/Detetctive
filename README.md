# 🔬 Forensic-X: Procedural Mystery Bot

Welcome to **Forensic-X**, an immersive, endless crime investigation and detective simulation built entirely in Python. Designed for both standalone CLI execution and Telegram bot deployment, this game challenges your analytical mind with dynamic, non-repeating cases.

## 🌟 Key Features

* **♾️ Infinite Procedural Alibis:** Dynamic alibi generation that combines randomized actions, time-slots, and alibi excuses to ensure every playthrough is completely unique.
* **🔬 Hardcore Forensic Dossiers:** Detailed pathological, tanatological, toxicological, and digital forensic reports featuring over **50+ professional terms** (including PMI, GC-MS, STR analysis, Dactyloscopy, Luminol reaction, and Chain of Custody).
* **🧩 Cryptographic & Logic Riddle Firewalls:** Access to crucial crime scene evidence and suspect alibis is securely locked behind an endless pool of procedural riddles.
* **🤖 Telegram Bot Integration:** Fully optimized for Telegram deployment, allowing single-player and multiplayer investigative sessions directly through bot commands.

## 🎮 Available Commands

* `/start` - Launch a brand new procedural forensic case and view suspect profiles.
* `/examine` - Unlock and review the detailed forensic autopsy and trace evidence reports (Riddle-locked).
* `/interrogate [Suspect Name]` - Question a suspect to reveal their dynamically generated alibi (Riddle-locked).
* `/arrest [Suspect Name]` - Submit your final deduction and arrest the mastermind.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Requirements

* **Language:** Python 3.x
* **Library:** `python-telegram-bot`
